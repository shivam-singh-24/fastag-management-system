Manage FASTag

Paytm FASTag is very easy to manage and updates you about all the transactions and low balance via SMS. Please visit "Manage FASTag" section in your FASTag sub-wallet on the Paytm App to find the list of tags linked to your wallet.

Manage FASTag section provides:

Tag-wise transaction details of all toll payments
Activation status of each FASTag that is linked to your wallet
Toggle button to enable Instant suspension/ reactivation* of your FASTag
